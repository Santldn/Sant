Luxury Interior Design Studio & Super Prime Property Developer

Inspired by over half a century of family expertise within real estate, brothers Alex and Michael Christou founded 1.61 London in 2010 which is based in Mayfair, London. The brand is dedicated to delivering truly exceptional, luxurious homes with the highest levels of design, craftmanship and service underpinned by a persistence and commitment to continually push the boundaries of what is possible.

Operating globally as a luxury Interior Design Studio and a super prime property developer – 1.61 London is able to offer a best in class, one-stop shop service to our clients, bringing together a unique set of in house skills to ensure the seamless delivery of the most exclusive global projects with an incredible amount of passion and attention to detail.

The brand has a global community of over 600,000 followers and has been accredited globally, securing multiple international design awards and listings as one of the worlds leading design names.

Client Services

FOR THOSE IN SEARCH OF A TAILOR MADE 360° SERVICE

We offer a full turnkey residential service to our clients from luxury interior design, architecture, planning, project management, construction and soft furnishing.

Property Sourcing

With the complications of the London property market many of our clients struggle to find the right home to purchase. As a unique service we use our in house knowledge of the London real estate market to source the right canvas for our clients that will allow us to build their dream home.

More Information

Luxury Interior Design

Whether the starting point is an existing home or as part of our property sourcing service - Our award winning design team will take our clients through the 1.61 London luxury interior design process which has been meticulously crafted offer 14 years to ensure we deliver the maximum amount of design detail and quality for our clients in the simplest and quickest format possible.

More Information

Architecture

Our RIBA qualified architectural team take over to ensure that the design we have created is viable with all the relevant stakeholders from planning, building control, fire regulations and license requirements from landlords where relevant. As part of this a set of construction issue drawings are created for our project team to tender from and these allow our construction team to ensure the exact delivery of each of our projects.

More Information

Project Management

The 1.61 London delivery team work with our luxury interior design and architectural teams throughout the design process to ensure that when it comes to the implementation of the project there is a seamless transition. A key aspect of this is ensuring that what is being designed by our team aligns with our clients initial budget estimate. We then utilise our vast array of trade contacts with the very best suppliers in the world to ensure our clients get the very best products for their home and budget.

More Information

Construction

Throughout construction our professional team work closely with the local neighbourhood to mitigate disruption to the local area and are repeatedly recognised by the Considerate Constructors Scheme for their exemplary standard of work.
