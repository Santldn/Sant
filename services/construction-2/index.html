FROM VISION TO REALITY

The 1.61 London in house construction and project teams are experts in ensuring a seamless construction process even in the hardest of neighborhoods. Managing and implementing complex new build projects through to delicately modernising and restoring heritage and listed properties whilst integrating intelligent solutions to maximise the end result.

We use our wide range of  skills and experience to ensure the very best implementation possible for our construction projects, from the materials utilised in the substructure, the best in class techniques used for our builds to the quality of finish that this delivers. We have spent years developing a close group of highly skilled craftman and specialists with a proven track record in the luxury property sector who we can work alongside enabling us to progress every project to an exacting standard in a timely fashion.

The Process

FOR THOSE WHO VALUE RARITY

An experience beyond the normal development process, delivering a 1.61 London home is as much about the effortless client journey as it is the final destination. Every project 1.61 London undertakes has been uniquely designed and our specialised tailored approach to construction through our best in class implementation team ensures consistent levels of final delivery.

Permissions

Working in parallel with our design team, the construction division look at the various permissions that are required for the project. There is the need to understand if planning and/or listed consents are required for the work that has been agreed. Alongside this a license for alterations may also be required depending on the ownership structure and location. All of these permissions can be lengthy and it's therefore important to run this aspect in parallel with the design process as much as is possible.

Site Set Up

Once we have agreed an implementation package through our interior design process - our team run through our pre-start procedures which includes various aspects from checking on asbestos, running health and safety, setting up a site office with welfare and ensuring all the correct insurances are in place.

Procurement

Managing the procurement process and cross-programme construction activity is vital to the delivery of any project. Our experienced team of project managers are consistently working between the design team and the construction team to ensure the right delivery of goods to site at the right time and checking that the implementation of the construction team is as expected. We have spent years growing a unique supplier network which ensures price competitiveness alsongisde the best products available in the market for our projects.

Construction

We have spent years building a group of highly skilled workers to ensure that the unique design visions of our creative team are implemented with the very best practices on offer within the design industry. Many of our builders are craftmen that are real specialists in their given area.

Completion

At completion the 1.61 London project team alongside the design team run a full internal snagging process to ensure that the project is ready for full handover to the client. We then ask our clients to run their own snagging process before move in so that all areas can be picked up. We ensure through this process that every detail of the project is exactly as expected so that our clients can move into their home with excitement for the end result.

Handover & Aftercare

A full handover of a new home takes place on day 1 alongside our handover book. We also offer all our clients an aftercare service so that any future problems can be dealt with by the same team who have implemented the project.
